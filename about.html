Manish Malhotra's prolific career spanning over 30 years has seen him redefine fashion as a couturier, costume stylist, entrepreneur, and revivalist. Manish began his career as a model, but his passion for movies and fashion soon led him to costume design.

Fashion, in my opinion, is an all-encompassing aesthetic that includes design, styling, and cosmetics. My passion for film allowed me to launch a brand while concurrently creating iconic looks for the big screen. I'm now living out in my childhood world being a director, bringing the magic of movies, fashion, and fun to life along with channeling my vision of embracing minimal maximalism fashion through my designs.
